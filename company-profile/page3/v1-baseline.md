## Section 4: Company Description

### English Version
```
PT Surya Inovasi Prioritas (SURIOTA) is a technology-based
company specializing in Industrial IoT Services and System
Integration, headquartered in Batam, Riau Islands, Indonesia.

We focus on developing and integrating Industrial Internet
of Things (IIoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) solutions
for the industrial, energy, logistics, and maritime sectors.

SURIOTA provides end-to-end consulting, integration, and
management services for industrial digital systems — combining
hardware, software, and intelligent analytics to help companies
enhance efficiency, reliability, and operational sustainability.
```

### Indonesian Version
```
PT Surya Inovasi Prioritas (SURIOTA) adalah perusahaan berbasis
teknologi yang bergerak di bidang Industrial IoT Services dan
System Integration, berkantor pusat di Batam, Kepulauan Riau,
Indonesia.

Kami berfokus pada pengembangan dan integrasi solusi Industrial
Internet of Things (IIoT) serta Artificial Intelligence (AI)
untuk sektor industri, energi, logistik, dan maritim.

SURIOTA menyediakan layanan konsultasi, integrasi, dan manajemen
sistem digital industrial secara end-to-end — mengombinasikan
hardware, software, dan intelligent analytics untuk membantu
perusahaan meningkatkan efisiensi, reliabilitas, dan
keberlanjutan operasional.
```
