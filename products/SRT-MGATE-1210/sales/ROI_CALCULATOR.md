## Bagian 4: Perhitungan ROI
## *Section 4: ROI Calculation*

### Formula

```
ROI (%) = (Total Penghematan - Total Investasi) / Total Investasi x 100%
ROI (%) = (Total Savings - Total Investment) / Total Investment x 100%

Periode Balik Modal (bulan) = Total Investasi / (Total Penghematan / 12)
Payback Period (months) = Total Investment / (Total Savings / 12)
```
